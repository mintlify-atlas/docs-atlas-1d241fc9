The entries are grouped alphabetically by first letter and sourced directly from `Dictionary/dictionary.tsx`.

<Info>
  The game shows only `explicacion` and `explicacion2` as hints during gameplay. The `explicacion3` field is included here for reference but is not shown to the player at any point during a session.
</Info>
